Historical Figures

Doctor Mokorai

Lived about 500 years ago. Cured cancer. Created the Ithrinian people. Is popular scapegoat for the beast wars.

Commander Koto Nogaila

Lived about 200 years ago. Took lead of Gailaborne through diplomatic tactics. First non-human elemental-warrior (fire); took the role by force. Preceded to use his power in combat against the humans. Usually targeted civilians and burnt many cities to the ground. His descendants still rule Gailaborne.

Iota Benja Nokonei

Lived about 200 years ago. First non-human elemental-warrior (time) who didn't take the role by force.
